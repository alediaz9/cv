Pasta Cooking Class

In a pasta cooking class, you'll learn how to make perfect pasta every time, from choosing the right type of pasta to cooking it to al dente perfection. You'll also learn how to make a variety of delicious pasta sauces, from classic marinara to creamy Alfredo, that will take your pasta dishes to the next level.

Private Online Cooking Classes

In a private online cooking class, you'll receive one-on-one instruction from a professional chef in the comfort of your own home. Together, you'll create a personalized menu and learn valuable culinary skills that will help you become a more confident and creative home cook.

Macaron Baking Class

In a macaron baking class, you'll learn the secrets to making perfect macarons, from creating the meringue to piping the delicate shells. You'll also learn to make a variety of flavorful fillings, such as buttercream and ganache, to create delicious and impressive macarons for any occasion.
